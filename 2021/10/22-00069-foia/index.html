22-00069-FOIA

October 12, 2021 | Suggest Changes

This request is for a transcript, meeting minutes, or a transcription of an electronic recording disclosing the identity of each speaker of the closed SEC meeting on May 13, 2021 @ 2 PM.

I filed this request after a strange series of events transpired in 2021:

April 13, 2021#

Commissioner Hester Peirce releases Token Safe Harbor Proposal 2.0 statement - github

May 11, 2021#

An article titled “The Future Of Tokenized Stocks - What They Can Replace And What To Watch Out For” was released.

Someone later edited the article to prominently feature SBF.

May 12, 2021#

Ryan Cohen posted this tweet from Arlington, VA GameStop, which is a 12-minute drive to the SEC.

Tokenized GME stocks are issued.

May 13, 2021#

There is a Closed Commission Meeting.

Sometime Between May and October#

I stumbled across this SEC regulation about closed Commission meetings.

I was new to the FOIA process at the time of request and missed the window for appeal. I submitted an identical request in 2022 that resulted in 22-03086-FOIA, which was appealed.
